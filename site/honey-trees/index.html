    INTRODUCTION

    Honey Tree Shiny Hunting Guide

    This guide will teach you how to efficiently shiny hunt Honey Trees, which are exclusive to the Sinnoh region.

    The route begins in Canalave City and ends near Sunyshore City.

    There are 21 Honey Trees in total; however, we recommend skipping the one on Route 221 (near Pal Park), as it’s typically not worth the travel time.

    Two Main Methods

    1. Run + Slather – quickly reapplying honey and moving on.
    2. Battle the Pokémon – lead with a Pokémon that knows Honey Gather, or a combo of Frisk + Thief.
